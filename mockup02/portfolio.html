The Paradigm Shift: AI in Pedagogy

Educational institutions are actively integrating artificial intelligence tools. While AI facilitates personalized learning pathways and accelerates cognitive acquisition, it simultaneously presents severe integrity risks like academic plagiarism. To mitigate these concerns, educators must establish critical-thinking guidelines, transforming AI into an intellectual catalyst rather than a cognitive crutch.
